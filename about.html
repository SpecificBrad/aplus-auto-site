About A+ Auto Centre

Family owned and operated since 2007, serving St. John's with honest automotive care

No Smoke & Mirrors. Just Honest Work.

At A+ Auto Centre, we believe in straightforward, honest automotive service. Since 2007, we've been serving the St. John's community with cost-effective, no-nonsense sales and service. We're not here to sell you things you don't need – we're here to keep you safely on the road.

As a family-owned business, we treat every customer like family. When you bring your vehicle to us, you're not just another number. You're working with professional automotive specialists who genuinely care about your safety and satisfaction.

Our Story

Founded in 2007, A+ Auto Centre was built on a simple principle: provide quality automotive service at fair prices, without the typical dealership markup and pressure tactics. Over the years, we've helped thousands of St. John's residents with everything from routine maintenance to finding their next reliable vehicle.

Located conveniently near Airport Heights at 77 Major's Path, we've become a trusted name in the community for both pre-owned vehicle sales and expert auto repair services.

What Sets Us Apart

Inspected & Certified Vehicles: Every vehicle we sell goes through a thorough inspection. We also offer As-Is options for budget-conscious buyers. Every sale includes a fresh oil change and full detail.

Professional Service: Our technicians are experienced automotive specialists who use only brand name parts. We don't cut corners – we do the job right the first time.

Transparent Pricing: No hidden fees. No surprise charges. We provide clear quotes and explain exactly what work needs to be done and why.

Fast Turnaround: We understand you need your vehicle. We work efficiently without sacrificing quality to get you back on the road as quickly as possible.
