# Vehicle Images Guide

## Image Requirements

### Recommended Specifications:
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Dimensions:** 800x600px (4:3 ratio) or 1200x800px
- **File size:** Under 200KB each (compressed)
- **Naming:** Use descriptive names like `2014-hyundai-elantra.jpg`

## How to Add Images:

### Option 1: Manual Download from NLClassifieds
1. Visit https://www.nlclassifieds.com/APlusAutoCentre
2. Right-click each vehicle image → "Save image as..."
3. Save to `/images/vehicles/` folder
4. Use the naming convention above

### Option 2: Stock Photos (Demo)
1. Go to Unsplash.com or Pexels.com
2. Search for each vehicle (e.g., "2014 Hyundai Elantra")
3. Download high-quality images
4. Rename and save to `/images/vehicles/`

### Option 3: Get Photos from Owner
After they agree to the project:
- Ask them to send photos of actual vehicles
- Or visit the lot and take photos yourself
- Professional touch: Take photos with your phone

## Image Optimization:

Before uploading to website:
1. **Resize:** Use TinyPNG.com or Squoosh.app
2. **Target:** Under 200KB per image
3. **This ensures:** Fast page loading
